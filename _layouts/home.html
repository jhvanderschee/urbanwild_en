Air purifying

Urban Wild plants purify the air in your home. In fact, these species were specifically chosen for their ability to clean the air. Our environment contains many toxic substances. Plants absorb and break down these substances in the air and convert CO2 into oxygen. They also improve the humidity in your home through the process of evaporation and absorb particulate matter in their leaves. All of this makes Urban Wild plants both beautiful and beneficial!
